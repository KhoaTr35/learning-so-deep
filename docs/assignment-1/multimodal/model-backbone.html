      Why this backbone

      Design rationale

      • The image tower is strong enough to separate the 10 selected food categories without full end-to-end fine-tuning.
      • The shared embedding space makes zero-shot prompting and few-shot adaptation directly comparable under one encoder.
      • Freezing CLIP lowers training cost and keeps the experiment focused on the classifier head decision, not backbone optimization.
      • The same embedding extractor powers the Streamlit demo and the offline experiment pipeline, which reduces product-model drift.
